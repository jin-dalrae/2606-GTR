Systems change

Embedding carbon accountability where founders already show up

Instead of asking individual startups to voluntarily measure impact, the assessment rides inside an accelerator’s existing intake — so it reaches every founder, in every cohort, by default.

Leverage
Pipeline
5 stages
Rae · Gabriel · Tej
Stage 01

Pitch to program directors

We introduce the work to program directors at climate accelerators, positioning it as a way to strengthen shared founder–investor understanding of climate impact.

Buy-in at the top, once.
Stage 02

Founder onboarding

A climate-specific accelerator adds a ~2-minute carbon assessment as a standard step in its existing application & onboarding — no new curriculum slot required.

One accelerator adopts the standard.
Stage 03

Identifying current progress

Founders see their company’s footprint/handprint generated. Simultaneously, program directors view the entire cohort on one platform.

Every founder, every cohort — automatically.
Stage 04

Preparation for Demo Day

Founders build their metrics into their pitch. PDs send investors a link before Demo Day, compiling the cohort’s assessment results.

One format, applied consistently across the cohort.
Stage 05

Communicating with investors

Founders present at Demo Day with the metrics. Investors see them across every pitch — and expect them in future cohorts.

Normalized across the entire investment pipeline.
One accelerator → One cohort → Many investors

One embedded step propagates the practice without depending on any single founder’s initiative.
