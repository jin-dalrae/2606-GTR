GTR Partners

Growth Through Responsibility

Accelerator for founders who want to build high-performance companies — not just high-growth ones.

We believe the best startups of the next decade will treat environmental performance as a core growth metric, not a side project. That’s why GTR Partners exists. We use a simple EI Dashboard (powered by Climatico) so founders measure, track, and improve Environmental Impact intensity from Day 1 — better decisions, stronger fundraising, real advantage.

The GTR commitment

  • Track EI intensity with revenue & users every month
  • Review environmental performance in team meetings as a standing item
  • Set & publicly share one clear 12-month EI reduction target
  • Design lower-impact choices into product, supply chain, operations
  • Use dashboard data with investors and talent
  • Share progress transparently with the cohort

What you get

  • 12-week intensive · weekly cohort sessions
  • Full EI Dashboard · onboarding & support
  • Mentorship from operators who already build this way
  • Intros to investors who use portfolio EI metrics
  • Peer accountability pods · monthly progress reviews
  • Optional public recognition for measurable improvement

Who this is for

  • Pre-seed → Series A founders
  • Want strength on financial and environmental metrics
  • Get ahead of investor & regulatory expectations
  • Attract mission-aligned capital and talent
  • Make impact a source of pride — not anxiety

Outcomes founders have achieved

18–25% average EI intensity cut in first 12 months
Funding multiple founders used dashboard data to close rounds
Culture stronger alignment around long-term value creation

This is not a greenwashing program. It’s for founders who want companies that are genuinely high-performing — financially, operationally, and environmentally.
