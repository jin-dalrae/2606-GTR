What is in the market

Free carbon calculators fall into three camps. None of them fully serve early-stage founders who need both honesty and a path to investor-grade maturity.

On this page

  1. Freemium platforms
  2. Institutional tools
  3. Cloud consoles
  4. Activity-based shift
  5. Verdict

1. Freemium lead generators

Enterprise ESG platforms ship free, lightweight tools to capture startup leads.

PlayersPlan A, Seedling, Sustain.Life, Normative (via SME Climate Hub), and similar.
ExperiencePolished UI. Simple inputs → pretty charts of estimated footprint.
CatchUsually Scope 1–2 only. Deeper Scope 3, automated bills, or verified exports hit a paywall.

2. Institutional baseline tools

PlayersUS EPA Simplified GHG Emissions Calculator; UC Berkeley CoolClimate.
ExperienceMacro-heavy Excel. Clunky. High data integrity via official factors (eGRID, etc.).
ValueAuditors respect the baseline. Startups often hate the workflow.

3. Built-in infrastructure dashboards

PlayersAWS Customer Carbon Footprint Tool, Google Cloud Carbon Footprint, Microsoft sustainability tiers.
ExperienceAccurate for digital footprint from billing; misses office, travel, and product handprint.

Market dynamics: activity-based data

Past (spend-based)“$5,000 on flights → X tonnes.” Distorted by price inflation.
Present (activity-based)Passenger-miles, kWh, tonne-km. Baselines that move when behavior changes.
